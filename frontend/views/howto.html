Welcome to Branch

Branch is an online multiplayer battle simulator, where commanders pit their recruits against each other. As the battle rages on, commanders can use Inversion Points (IP) to evolve their recruits and unlock special abilities.

To begin, click the “Battle” at the bottom of this page, or on the home screen to enter the lobby, where you will be matched with a random player.

Note: If you make an account, you can also challenge your friends by entering each other’s username on the challenge screen!

When entering a battle, you may choose any combination of the three types of class trees shown below. Each tree's third tier unit has a unique, unlockable ability.

After choosing your recruits, the battle begins. Note the A (Attack) and S (Speed) values on the top left of each recruit card. Attack is the amount of base damage each recruit will do and speed is the order in which recruits will attack. Ties in speed result in random ordering.

Hover over your recruits to choose its action. The top button, attack, allows you to pick your target enemy. The bottom button, defend, applies a 2/3 damage modifier to any incoming damage regardless of type.

There are three types of recruits: Future (Blue), Feudal (Red), and Fantasy (Green). Red beats Green, Green beats Blue, and Blue beats Red.

Stronger types deal 1.5 times damage against a weaker type, matched types take and deal equal damage, and weaker types deal 2/3 damage.
