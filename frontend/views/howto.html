Welcome to Branch

Branch is an online multiplayer battle simulator, where commanders pit their recruits against other commanders. As the battle rages, commanders can use IP (Inversion Points) to evolve their units and unlock special abilities.

When entering a battle, you must choose any combination of the three types of class trees. Each tree's third-stage unit has a unique ability that can be unlocked.

After choosing your units, the battle begins. Hover over your recruits to make the choice as to either attack or defend.

Be sure to take into consideration that each type has a counter. Also note, every upgraded unit also has a counter.
